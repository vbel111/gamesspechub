Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate are the system requirements?

+

Our system requirements are sourced from official game developers and verified through community feedback. We also provide real-world performance data to give you a better understanding of how games will run on your system.

Is GameSpecsHub free to use?

+

Yes! GameSpecsHub is completely free to use. We believe everyone should have access to information that helps them make informed gaming decisions.

How often is the game database updated?

+

Our database is updated daily with new games, patches, and system requirement changes. We use the RAWG API to ensure we have the most current information available.

Can I suggest games to be added?

+

We welcome game suggestions from our community. You can contact us through this form or our social media channels with your recommendations.

Do you have a mobile app?

+

Currently, GameSpecsHub is a web-based platform optimized for all devices. We're working on a dedicated mobile app that will be available soon!

How can I report incorrect information?

+

If you notice incorrect system requirements or game information, please contact us immediately. We take data accuracy seriously and will investigate and correct any issues promptly.
